# Part 1: Brand Positioning

## 01. Brand Journey Framework

**Objective:** Reverse engineer your brand from the destination back to today.

### Exercise

**1. What is my desired outcome?**
*What do I ultimately want my brand to accomplish for me or my business?*



**2. What do I need to be known for in order for that to happen?**
*If I want that outcome, what must people immediately associate me with?*



**3. What do I need to do in order to be known for that?**
*What actions must I consistently take to reinforce that reputation?*



**4. What do I need to learn right now to make that possible?**
*What skills or knowledge am I missing today that will allow me to follow through?*



> **Note:** This is your brand's north star. Come back to it often.

# Quick Reference

## Content That Works Long-Term
- Consistency beats intensity
- Sustainability beats optimization
- Direction without execution goes nowhere
- Execution without direction burns people out

## Key Questions to Revisit
1. Does this align with my Brand Journey Framework?
2. Am I building a prison or a platform?
3. Is this sustainable for 12-18 months?
4. Am I being honest about my stage (expert vs student)?
